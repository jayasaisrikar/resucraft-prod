About Our Project

Welcome to ResuCraft, our innovative Resume Builder born out of ambitious full-stack development projects. We harnessed the power of Django for the backend, utilized PostgreSQL as our database, and crafted a sleek and responsive user interface with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

Our Team's Contributions

Eswar took charge of designing the Django framework, handling integration, establishing database connectivity, and conducting project testing. He also authored code for models and views, enabling users to edit and add new information. Srikar collaborated with Eswar, assisting in database management, administration, testing, and contributing to pages like login and signup. Vivek focused on creating an interactive UI for various pages, including the homepage, about page, contact page, and templates page. Finally, Bhavani contributed by designing some of the templates

User-Focused Goals

Effortlessly navigate our website through a seamless login or sign-up process. Input new data securely stored in our database, readily accessible in user profiles, and seamlessly integrated into our stylish resume templates. Customize your resumes with ease using a user-friendly form, allowing for editing or addition of new information. Lastly, download refined resumes in PDF format, selecting from a variety of professionally designed templates.
